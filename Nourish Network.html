Faq (Frequently Asked Questions)

How does this platform connect surplus food donors with those in need?

Our platform utilizes a web-based architecture that allows surplus food donors to list their available items, which are then matched with individuals or organizations in need through our network.

What types of organizations can become surplus food donors?

Any organization that deals with surplus food, such as restaurants, grocery stores, event venues, and caterers, can become donors on our platform.

Is there a fee for organizations to use your platform as donors?

No, our platform is free for organizations to use as donors. We believe in facilitating food donation without financial barriers.

How do individuals or organizations in need access the surplus food listed on your platform?

Individuals or organizations in need can register on our platform and browse the available surplus food listings. They can then request the items they require, which are subsequently coordinated for pickup or delivery.

How do you ensure the quality and safety of the surplus food being donated?

We provide guidelines and best practices for food handling and safety to our donors. Additionally, our platform encourages transparency, and users can leave reviews and ratings, ensuring accountability.

Can surplus food donors specify dietary restrictions or preferences for the items they donate?

Yes, donors can specify details about the surplus food they are offering, including dietary restrictions, expiration dates, and any other relevant information.
